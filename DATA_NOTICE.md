# Data Notice

This repository includes a frozen analysis copy of the SPARC rotmod files and SPARC Table1 metadata used by the manuscript, plus derived residual, control, and labeling artifacts.

Primary SPARC citation:

Lelli, F., McGaugh, S. S., & Schombert, J. M. 2016, AJ, 152, 157, "SPARC: Mass Models for 175 Disk Galaxies with Spitzer Photometry and Accurate Rotation Curves", doi:10.3847/0004-6256/152/6/157.

The copied SPARC files are included to make the paper packet reproducible at the exact paths cited by the manuscript. Downstream users should also cite the original SPARC data release and follow the original data-use terms.
