# Data Notice

This repository includes derived residual, control, and labeling artifacts used by the manuscript. It does not redistribute the raw SPARC rotmod files or SPARC Table1 metadata; those inputs are fetched by `studies/sparc_residual_coherence_test_v01/download_sparc_data.py` from the public SPARC Zenodo archive.

Primary SPARC citation:

Lelli, F., McGaugh, S. S., & Schombert, J. M. 2016, AJ, 152, 157, "SPARC: Mass Models for 175 Disk Galaxies with Spitzer Photometry and Accurate Rotation Curves", doi:10.3847/0004-6256/152/6/157.

SPARC Zenodo archive:

Lelli, F., McGaugh, S. S., & Schombert, J. M. 2016, "SPARC Galaxy Rotation Curve", Zenodo, doi:10.5281/zenodo.16284118.

The Zenodo record lists the dataset under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International. Downstream users should cite the original SPARC paper and the Zenodo data release, and should follow the original data-use terms.
