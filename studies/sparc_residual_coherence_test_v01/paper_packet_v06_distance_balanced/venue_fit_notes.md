# Venue Fit Notes

## Best Framing

```text
Phenomenological / methodological audit of residual-disturbance structure in SPARC.
```

Avoid framing as:

```text
new gravity proof
Tau Core proof
dark matter falsification
MOND falsification
projection-formula uniqueness test
```

## Plausible Venues

### Research Notes of the AAS

Fit:

```text
Short, compact empirical note with a narrow result and strong reproducibility.
```

Tradeoff:

```text
Length constraints likely require most appendices to become data/code supplement.
```

### Galaxies / Universe

Fit:

```text
More room for methods, appendices, and phenomenological interpretation.
```

Tradeoff:

```text
Needs careful tone and standard literature framing to avoid appearing as a theory-first paper.
```

### ApJ-style Short Note

Fit:

```text
Scientifically possible only if the paper is tightened around residual-disturbance phenomenology.
```

Tradeoff:

```text
Likely reviewers will demand independent validation or stronger observability metadata.
```

## Recommended First Submission Shape

```text
Title: External structural disturbance predicts low-acceleration rotation-curve residual scatter in SPARC
Main text: concise audit result
Supplement: row-level labels, baseline tables, regression, systematics, S_tau exploratory bridge
Cover letter: emphasize residual-blind labels, reproducibility, and cautious claims
```
